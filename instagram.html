This is my project - Instagram Stories

Over a span of 837 days, I have been actively capturing photographs, resulting in a total of 1845 pictures, which equates to an average of more than 2 photos per day. These photographs were consistently shared as stories on my Instagram account. Although stories may disappear over time, these photos have been saved and are now accessible for your viewing pleasure. It is important to note that I have carefully selected and curated my collection due to privacy concerns, and have decided to showcase only my top 1359 photos. These photos truly represent my love for photography, and I hope you enjoy them as much as I do!
